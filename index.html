They Will Kill You is a 2026 action-horror-comedy film directed by Kirill Sokolov and starring Zazie Beetz, Tom Felton, and Heather Graham. The plot follows an ex-convict who takes a live-in housekeeping job at a mysterious New York City high-rise, only to discover she has walked into a dangerous community harboring a Satanic cult.
